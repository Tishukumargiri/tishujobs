Built by a Fresher,
for Every Fresher

My name is Tishu Kumar Giri, and I'm a passionate aspiring Software Developer from India. Like millions of freshers, I struggled to find my first opportunity. Existing platforms were either too complex, too expensive, or simply not built with freshers in mind.

"Every fresher deserves equal access to opportunities, regardless of which college they attended or city they're from. Technology should be the great equalizer."

I created TishuJobs to solve a real problem I faced myself. A platform specifically designed for students, fresh graduates, and first-time job seekers — complete with AI-powered tools, a supportive community, and thousands of verified opportunities.

My vision is simple: to build India's most trusted platform for freshers and entry-level professionals. A place where your potential matters more than your experience.

🎯

Our Mission

Helping freshers build successful careers by connecting them with the right opportunities, tools, and community support at every step of their journey.

🚀

Our Vision

To become India's leading fresher-focused job platform — where every student has equal access to their dream career, powered by AI and community.

💡

Our Values

Inclusivity, transparency, and empowerment. Every feature we build asks: "Does this genuinely help a fresher land their first job?"
